Who We Are

Spectrum Protective Systems (SPS) A Family-Owned Company

Fully licensed and insured, SPS services all of Nassau and Suffolk counties. From Malvern to Montauk and Fire Island to the Long Island Sound we've got you covered.

SPS can furnish your family and business with the best and most affordable Security and Fire detection systems available. We deal with the latest state-of-the-art, "industry standard" equipment available. We can provide you with a myriad of different systems and service packages to meet every need and budget.

SPS stands behind every system we install, giving you the best in quality, service, and peace of mind. Whether Commercial or residential, our experienced staff can custom design any alarm system that fits your needs at a guaranteed low price. We tackle all size burglary alarm jobs and commercial fire alarm systems as well.
