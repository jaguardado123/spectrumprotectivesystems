WHAT WE DO

Burglary Detection

Every security system we install is specifically designed for the business or residence that it protects.

With a wide variety of great products from ELK to Honeywell, we are sure to have a burglar alarm or security product that will help us protect you and your family from theft, for a well-deserved peace of mind. SPS provides and services all sizes of residences and commercial premises as well. The security systems we install are filled with communications and connectivity features such as:

  • State of the art hardwired & wireless systems
  • Flexibility in alarm reporting, supporting IP, cellular, and landline
  • Automatic E-mail alerts of select system events
  • Outdoor detection systems
  • Remote keypad control of burglary systems
  • Remote connectivity for easy user interface and programing, as well as convenient system control though popular smart devices
  • Intelligent integration and automation of lights, thermostats, garage doors, gates, sprinklers, water valves and more

Fire Alarm Systems

Fire alarms provide life safety as well as property protection to your home or business, schools and places of worship.

Billions of dollars are lost by businesses to fire each year.

Fire alarm systems are such an essential area of commercial security protection!

SPS skilled team of security experts plan, design, and install the most comprehensive fire alarm systems in Nassau County and Suffolk County. These systems protect thousands of people, along with millions of dollars in facilities, inventory, and assets each year.

Our fire detection systems offer solutions for the quick detection of fire or smoke, allowing you time to organize an orderly, efficient evacuation of employees and customers. Early detection can also help alleviate property loss and damage by expediting a quicker response from local fire fighters. Our fire detection systems are tailored to the needs of each individual business with features that promote quick and comprehensive detection and response.

Our commercial fire alarm system services include:

  • Complete Fire Alarm Installations, Retrofits, and Replacements
  • Annual Inspections and Service Plans
  • ADA Compliance Designs and Notification
  • NFPA Compliance Systems and Designs
